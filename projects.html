Project Frontier

As a Business Analyst my role was to endure to ensure all requirements were documented in a manner the was understandable by the finance teams and the IT technicians for the Record to Report tower. International collaboration was essential when working with the technician in India and the financial teams in EMEA, US, ANZ and ASEAN. Technologies included OCR, MQ, XML, SFTP and AS400.

Project Butterfly

A third-party service provider was employed to perform the majority of the development work for the Japanese, APAC and Benelux territories. I acted as a consultant for the Finance and Role Based Security modules within JD Edwards World as well as Robot scheduler and the IBM Websphere MQ interfaces. Due to the size of the operation in Japan I designed new role security configuration which I delivered in person in Japan. The Japanese office requested I return to Japan to provide the onsite go-live support. An incredible experience.

Project Chrysalis

As IT Finance Stream Lead, I identified gaps which needed resolutions to enable the finance teams to perform their tasks using Vanilla JD Edwards. I designed and developed the Self Bill process which allowed matching, at line level, the self bill invoices from the customer to the actual shipments from the warehouse giving the Order to Cash team full visibility of outstanding despatches for the first time. A second role on the project was to work with the business to translate their business roles into role-based security within JD Edwards.
